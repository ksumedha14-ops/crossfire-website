Do I need a separate Google Business Profile for each Gurgaon branch?

Yes. Google treats each physical location as an independent entity with its own ranking signals. A business with a DLF Phase 4 location and a Sohna Road branch needs two separately optimised GBP profiles to rank in both areas. Running both from a single profile means you will rank in neither location consistently. We manage multi-location Gurgaon setups as standard, with geo-specific content for each zone.

My business is on Justdial and Sulekha but invisible on Google Maps — why?

Justdial and Google Maps are entirely separate systems. Reviews, enquiries, and traffic on Justdial have zero influence on your Google Maps ranking. Google ranks businesses based on GBP optimisation, citation consistency, review velocity, and proximity signals — none of which Justdial activity feeds into. Gurgaon businesses that are strong on Justdial but invisible on Google Maps are one of our most common starting points, and one of the fastest turnarounds we see.

Is Local SEO better than Google Ads for a Gurgaon service business?

For most Gurgaon service businesses, Local SEO produces a stronger long-term return because Maps rankings are free and compound — each improvement holds after it is made. Google Ads generate immediate visibility but stop the moment you pause spending, and cost-per-click in competitive Gurgaon categories is high. The strongest approach combines both, but if budget requires a choice, Local SEO builds the foundation that generates enquiries without a daily ad spend. We can advise on the right mix during the free audit.
