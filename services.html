Meta Ads + Google Ads Management

Paid acquisition on both
platforms, fully managed.

Running paid ads profitably on two platforms requires daily monitoring, continuous testing, and deep familiarity with both Meta and Google's constantly changing systems. A competent in-house ads person costs ₹30,000–₹50,000/month in salary alone — before tools, onboarding time, and supervision. We manage both platforms for you: Google Search Ads capture customers already searching for your service; Meta Ads build demand from people in your area who haven't searched yet. Your ad spend goes directly to the platforms — not through us — so there's no markup on what you actually spend.

What's included

  • Campaign strategy: which keywords to bid on, which audiences to target, which offer converts best for your market
  • Google Search Ads: keyword research, negative keyword management, bid strategy, and ad copy testing every month
  • Meta Ads: Facebook & Instagram campaigns targeting your exact service area and customer profile
  • Ad creative written and refreshed monthly — copy, imagery direction, and A/B tests
  • Retargeting sequences for everyone who clicked but didn't enquire
  • Negative keyword management so your budget isn't wasted on irrelevant searches
  • Weekly report: spend by platform, cost per lead, which ads are winning and which are cut
  • You own the ad accounts — if you ever leave, you keep the full history and data
