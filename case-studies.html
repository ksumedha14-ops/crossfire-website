Featured HVAC / Home Services · South Delhi

From #8 on Google Maps to
#2 — in eight weeks.

The Situation

Kumar HVAC & Plumbing had been operating in South Delhi for 6 years. Word-of-mouth kept them busy, but their online presence was nearly invisible. They ranked #8 for their primary keyword, had 11 reviews, and their Google Business Profile was only 38% complete. Meanwhile, three competitors were ranking above them — two of which had launched within the last 18 months.

What We Did

  • Complete GBP audit — found 14 critical missing fields, wrong service categories, and outdated hours
  • Citation cleanup — fixed 23 inconsistencies across 38 directories, removed 2 duplicate listings
  • Review generation system — automated follow-up sequence generating 4–6 new reviews per week
  • Keyword repositioning — identified 6 high-intent terms competitors weren't targeting
  • Photo strategy — 28 professional-quality photos uploaded with optimised file names and alt text
  • Monthly competitor monitoring to detect ranking threats early
