Why home service businesses don't rank
✗
Wrong GBP category. Listed as "Contractor" or "Home improvement" instead of "HVAC Contractor", "Plumber", or "Electrician" — the categories buyers actually search.
✗
NAP inconsistency. Phone number or address differs across Justdial, Sulekha, IndiaMart, and the website — Google's trust signal drops, ranking follows.
✗
Service area misconfigured. Many home service businesses set a storefront address when they should be configured as a service-area business — causing ranking to miss the localities they actually serve.
✗
Justdial reviews that don't transfer. Strong reputation on Justdial has zero influence on Google Maps. Customers who would leave you a review need a Google-specific ask at the right moment.
✗
Duplicate GBP listings. A common legacy problem — old agencies or directory aggregators created multiple GBP entries for the same business, splitting ranking signals.

Common Questions

Questions from HVAC & home service business owners

Why isn't my HVAC or plumbing business showing up for emergency searches on Google Maps?

Emergency searches like "AC repair near me" or "emergency plumber Delhi" are won by the business with the most complete and consistent Google Business Profile — not the oldest or most experienced one. The three most common causes: wrong GBP category (listed as "Contractor" instead of a specific trade), citation inconsistencies where your phone number varies across Justdial, Sulekha, and other directories, and fewer than 10 recent Google reviews. All three are fixable within the first month.

How is Local SEO for home services different from general Local SEO?

Home service businesses — HVAC, plumbing, electrical — are "service area businesses" in Google's terminology. Unlike a clinic or salon where customers come to you, you go to them. This requires a different GBP setup: hiding your home address, configuring a service area covering your localities, and using the correct trade-specific GBP category. Getting this configuration wrong means you either don't rank at all or rank in the wrong area.

We have different phone numbers listed across Justdial, Sulekha, and our website. How much does this hurt?

Significantly. Google cross-references your business information across the web to verify legitimacy. When your phone number on Justdial differs from your GBP, Google's confidence in your business drops — which directly suppresses your Maps ranking. Citation cleanup is consistently one of the fastest-moving ranking improvements we make for home service clients. Kumar HVAC had 23 citation inconsistencies fixed in the first month; it contributed to moving from #8 to #2 in 8 weeks.

We're already on Justdial and Sulekha with good reviews. Why do we still need Google?

Justdial and Google Maps are entirely separate systems. Your Justdial reviews have zero influence on your Google Maps ranking. When a customer searches "HVAC near me" or "plumber in Dwarka" on Google, your Justdial rating is invisible. The faster a search leads to a phone call — which is true for almost every home service search — the more Google Maps visibility matters over Justdial. We specialise in converting existing Justdial reputation into Google reviews.

How long does it take to rank on Google Maps for home service keywords in Delhi NCR?

Most HVAC, plumbing, and electrical businesses in Delhi NCR see measurable ranking movement within 4–8 weeks of fixing the core issues — GBP category, citation inconsistencies, and initial review generation. Kumar HVAC (South Delhi) moved from #8 to #2 in 8 weeks; Sharma Electricals (Gurgaon) reached #1 for 3 primary keywords in 9 weeks. We include a 60-day progress guarantee on every engagement.

Do I need separate GBP listings for HVAC, plumbing, and electrical if we offer all three?

One GBP per physical business location. You don't need separate listings per service — but you do need to choose a primary GBP category wisely (whichever service generates the most revenue and has the strongest search volume for your area) and add the others as secondary categories. Splitting into multiple GBPs for the same address risks duplicate listing penalties. We advise on the right category structure in the free audit.

Is Local SEO or Google Ads better for emergency-intent home service searches?

Local SEO produces the stronger long-term return. Google Ads generate immediate calls but stop the moment you pause spending — and cost-per-click for "emergency plumber" and "AC repair" terms in Delhi NCR is high. Maps organic rankings cost nothing per click once earned. Emergency-intent searches have extremely high conversion rates because the caller is ready to book immediately. The strongest approach combines both, but Maps ranking builds the asset that works without daily ad spend.
